TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

On August 31, 2022, my sister, cousins and I went on vacation to Vietnam with a travel agency. We were there for 4 days 3 nights and stayed at a hotel in Ho Chi Minh city @ Saigon. The city is very dense with many people and buildings. There are so many Vietnamese people that the roads are always full of cars and motorbikes. In Ho Chi Minh there are Muslims and also halal restaurants and several mosques. So we don't have to worry about the food served. There, there are many cafes that sell coffee. I have tried Vietnamese coffee and it is very fragrant and delicious. While there, we tried a lot of new things in Vietnam, such as riding a rickshaw in the middle of the road full of vehicles, riding a canoe on the Mekong river, and going down an underground tunnel.
